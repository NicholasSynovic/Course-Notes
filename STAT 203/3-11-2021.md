## Poisson Process

- A poisson proces random variable counting x occurrences at time *t*
- v(x) = x * t
  - *x* is rate
  - *t* is time

## Poisson Approximation of Binomial

- Normal binomial probability
  - p(x) = (n! / x!(n - x)!) * (p^x) * (1 - p)^(n - x)
- Poisson Approximation
  - p(x) = (e^-u * u^e) / x!
