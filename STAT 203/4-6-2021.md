## Recap

- Covarience of X and Y is a measure of association
  - Cov(X, Y) = E(XY) - E(Y) * E(Y)

## Covarience

- Examples
  - Cov (X, Y) = E(xy) - E(x) * E(y)
  - E(xy) = double integral xy * f(x , y) dx dy
